Orange

The orange is a juicy citrus fruit known for its sweet and tangy taste. It is rich in Vitamin C, which helps boost immunity and keeps the skin healthy. Oranges are eaten fresh, made into juice, or used in desserts and jams. Grown widely in tropical regions, they are refreshing, nutritious, and loved by people of all ages.

Mango

The mango, known as the "King of Fruits," is a tropical fruit loved for its sweet taste and fragrance. It is rich in Vitamin A and Vitamin C, which are good for health and immunity. Mangoes are eaten fresh or used in juices, milkshakes, pickles, and desserts. Grown mainly in India and other warm regions, they are a favorite summer delight.

Apple

The apple is a popular fruit known for its crisp texture and sweet or slightly tangy flavor. It is rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants that help keep the body healthy. Apples can be eaten fresh or used in juices, pies, jams, and salads. Grown in many parts of the world, they are often called a symbol of good health.

Banana

The banana is a soft, sweet fruit that is widely eaten across the world. It is rich in potassium, vitamins, and fiber, which give energy and support good digestion. Bananas can be eaten fresh or used in milkshakes, cakes, and snacks. Easy to carry and peel, they are a healthy and quick source of nutrition.

Kiwi

Kiwi is a small, brown-skinned fruit with bright green flesh and tiny black seeds. It is rich in Vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants that boost immunity and aid digestion. Kiwi has a sweet and tangy flavor, making it perfect for juices, salads, and desserts. Though small in size, it is packed with great nutritional value.

Pineapple

The pineapple is a tropical fruit with a rough spiky skin and sweet, juicy yellow flesh. It is rich in Vitamin C, manganese, and antioxidants that support digestion and boost immunity. Pineapple is eaten fresh or used in juices, desserts, and salads. Its refreshing taste makes it a favorite summer fruit worldwide.

Pomogranate

The pomegranate is a round fruit with a tough red skin and many juicy, ruby-red seeds inside. It is rich in antioxidants, Vitamin C, and fiber, which promote good health and immunity. Pomegranates are eaten fresh or used in juices, salads, and desserts. Their sweet-tart flavor and health benefits make them very popular.

Strawberry

The strawberry is a bright red, heart-shaped fruit known for its sweet and juicy taste. It is rich in Vitamin C, antioxidants, and fiber, which help improve health and immunity. Strawberries are enjoyed fresh or used in jams, juices, cakes, and ice creams. Loved worldwide, they are both delicious and highly nutritious.
